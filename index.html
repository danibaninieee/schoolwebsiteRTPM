About Our School

RTPM-DSHS (Ramon Teves Pastor Memorial– Dumaguete Science High School) is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education in science, technology, and the arts. Since its establishment, the school has been committed to nurturing young minds to become responsible, innovative, and community-oriented leaders. The school boasts a rigorous curriculum that challenges students to excel academically while also developing their critical thinking and problem-solving skills. With highly qualified teachers, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a well-equipped library, RTPM-DSHS ensures that students have access to the best resources to support their learning. Beyond academics, the school encourages participation in extracurricular activities, from science fairs and math competitions to sports and cultural programs, helping students discover their passions and build confidence.

RTPM-DSHS has earned a reputation for excellence, consistently producing top-performing students in regional and national competitions. The school has received numerous awards for academic achievement, innovation, and community service, reflecting its commitment to holistic education. Central to the school's philosophy is the belief that education should not only focus on knowledge but also on character development. The values of integrity, diligence, teamwork, and respect are instilled in every student, preparing them to contribute positively to society. Graduates of RTPM-DSHS are well-prepared to pursue higher education and succeed in their chosen fields, demonstrating the school's enduring mission to cultivate future leaders who are both knowledgeable and socially responsible.

Join the RTPM-DSHS community today and be part of an environment that fosters learning, growth, and excellence. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or supporter, explore our programs, attend our events, and see why RTPM-DSHS continues to lead in education and innovation.
